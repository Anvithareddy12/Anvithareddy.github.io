About
Engineer, not experimenter.
I don't build demos. I build systems that teams depend on every day.

At AMD, I own the AI assistant that replaced manual SQL workflows across the org. Enterprise teams query messy data in plain English — I built the agentic RAG pipeline, the Text-to-SQL layer over Snowflake, and the eval framework that catches hallucinations before users see them.

Before that, I built a multilingual conversational AI platform from scratch at CornerstoneX — 50+ languages, CUDA-optimized to sub-2-second latency, integrated with Metahuman avatars. Presented the work at NVIDIA GTC 2025.

I also shipped an ASL gesture recognition system that handles 1,000+ signs at 95%+ accuracy in real-time. It was approved for pilot testing by the U.S. Department of Homeland Security for deployment in airports and transit hubs.
